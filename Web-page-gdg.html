Women Techmakers

A community by Google aimed at empowering women in technology through resources, events, and networking opportunities.

What is WTM?

Women Techmakers is Google's global program that provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology. It aims to create a world where all women can thrive in tech through workshops, networking events, and leadership opportunities.

Why join a tech community?

Joining a tech community like WTM offers numerous benefits:

  • Mentorship: Guidance from experienced professionals
  • Collaborative Projects: Hands-on experience working with others
  • Confidence Building: Speaking and leadership opportunities
  • Networking: Connect with like-minded individuals
